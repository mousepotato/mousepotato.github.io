一. The ten non-English words that were voted hardest to translate

1. ilunga (Bantu language of Tshiluba for "a person who is ready to forgive any abuse for the first time, to tolerate it a second time, but never a third time"). 希鲁巴语,世人愿意以德报怨两次，但决不会有第三次 .


2. shlimazl (Yiddish for a chronically unlucky person) 依第语,一个时时都倒霉人


3.radioukacz (Polish for a person who worked as a telegraphist for the resistance movements on the Soviet side of the Iron Curtain), however it has been proven not to be a real word and it's either a mistake or a hoax. 波兰语,在铁幕苏联进行抵抗运动的电讯员


4.naa (Japanese word only used in the Kansai area of Japan, especially in Osaka (大阪府), to emphasize statements or agree with someone). 日本语,只流行于关西,用作强调语气或表示赞同的语气助词


5.altahmam (Arabic for a kind of deep sadness) 阿拉伯语,沉痛哀伤的一种


6.gezellig (Dutch for cosy) 荷兰语,舒适


7.saudade (Portuguese for a certain type of longing) 葡萄牙语,渴望的一种


8.selathirupavar (Tamil for a certain type of truancy) 泰米尔语,逃学的一种


9.pochemuchka (Russian for a person who asks a lot of questions) 俄语,一个每件事都要问的人


10. klloshar (Albanian for loser) 阿尔巴尼亚语,失败

二.The ten English words that were voted hardest to translate

1.plenipotentiary

由 plenus + potens, full + power 构成.有两个意思:

a).做名词:一个拥有全权的人,通常指代一个代替政府部门的外交官或者特使.

b).做形容词:拥有多权利的


2.gobbledegook

指代冗繁而难解的文字或语言,官样文章.

一个好玩的东东,在英语中,当人们对很难理解一句话或者一个语言的时候通常会说,"It's all Greek to me"或 "talking double Dutch",但在Greek,人们通常会说,"he is said to speak in Arabic"或者"You are speaking chinese"
